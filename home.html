The Man of Many Talents

Kim Schultz is man of many talents. He has travelled to almost every county in the state of Nebraska and has almost been to all 50 states. He was happily married until cancer took his wife and raised 4 boys. He loves woodworking, hunting, and fishing. His true passion is horseback riding. From a young age, he loved everything about riding horses. His passion would lead him to meet new friends, try competitive sports, and work on a motion picture.
